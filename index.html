Hi there!

I'm Ruotong, a second-year Master's student at the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI). I'm currently working on my Master's Thesis with Dr. Nazanin Andalibi on wellbeing interventions on social media. My research interests lie broadly in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), including accessibility (assistive technology), critical algorithm studies, wearable technology, and disability studies.

Prior to starting my Master's degree, I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Technology with a minor in Industrial Design at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). My Bachelor's Thesis focuses on self-supervised audio embedding for depression detection. I was affiliated with Advanced Network Laboratory (ANL) where I was working on improving diversity in the recommendation system with Dr. Xiaofeng Gao.
